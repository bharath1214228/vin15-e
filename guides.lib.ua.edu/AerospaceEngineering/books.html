    A collection of electronic engineering reference books covering the following fields: biomedical, chemical, civil, communications, construction, electrical, energy, environmental, green/sustainable, industrial, materials science, mechanical, nanotechnology, optical.

Selected Ebooks

  • The Standard Handbook for Aeronautical and Astronautical Engineers by Mark Davies
    ISBN: 9780071362290
    Publication Date: 2002-10-31
  • Smithsonian Physical Tables by William Elmer Forsythe (Compiled by)
    ISBN: 9781591245308
    Publication Date: 2003-01-01
    This classic reference source comprises 901 tables of common physical and chemical data. The information provided is broad in scope. It is of general interest to scientists and engineers, and of particular interest to those involved with physics in its larger sense.
